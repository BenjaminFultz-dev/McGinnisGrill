Meet Chef John...

McGinnis’ Grill is a mobile food truck in Columbus, OH, with a focus on gourmet sandwiches. John McGinnis and his wife, Katie, met each other working at a local restaurant and share a love of trying new restaurants and food. Chef John has worked his entire life in various food settings, from concession stands as a child to fine dining. Sandwiches and comfort food are where he found his passion.

He loves cooking for family and friends, and the sandwiches are named after special people in his life who have spent years inspiring and trying his creations. Katie is a kindergarten teacher in Hilliard, but will be working on the truck in the summer. You will find the truck hopping around Columbus, especially in the Clintonville area.

McGinnis’ Grill is aiming to set the bar for food trucks in Ohio, with quality ingredients and service, unique flavors, and food that keeps you coming back for more. Their love of good food and serving the community will be evident when you visit McGinnis’ Grill!
